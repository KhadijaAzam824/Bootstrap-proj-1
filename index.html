Our Chefs

Our experts all have 5+ years working experiance as a Chef in the market

Zakir Qureshi

Muhammad Zakir Qureshi better known as Chef Zakir is a Pakistani television chef, host, hotelier and food personality who has appeared on cooking programs on Indus TV, Hum TV and Masala TV. He is an expert on Pakistani cuisine as well as international cuisine. Wikipedia Born:February 16, 1967 (age 57 years), KarachiParents:Abdul Aziz Qureshi Cooking style:Pakistani Cuisine,Asian cuisine, South African cuisine

Zarnak Sidhwa

Zarnak Sidhwa is a Pakistani chef and chocolatier. She is featured in Hum Masala TV show Food Diaries, and used to be a part of Chocoholics. She is the daughter in law of Pakistan's Supreme Court's Justice Rustam S. Sidhwa. She belongs to Parsi community of Karachi

Mehboob Khan

Mehboob Khan better known as Chef Mehboob is a Pakistani chef, a television personality and a cooking expert. He is known for his cooking shows Zauq Zindagi and Good Healthy Life on ARY Zauq. He is also an author of cookbook Food for Life. He served as a Judge in cooking competition MasterChef Pakistan.

Samia Ahad

Samia Ahad is a Pakistani chef who is head chef at the Coriander Leaf restaurant in Singapore, having previously worked in restaurants in Manhattan. Ahad opened a second restaurant, inside her Screening Room entertainment complex
